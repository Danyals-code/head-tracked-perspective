3D Shelf

A head-tracked window into your monitor

Your screen becomes a shallow room. As you move your head, off-axis perspective makes the objects inside feel anchored behind the glass.

  • Runs entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded
  • Desktop or laptop with a webcam works best
  • Sit 30–80 cm away, well-lit, facing the screen

Camera permission is required. Once inside — F fullscreen, R recenter, S settings.

Troubleshooting
  • Grant camera permission for this site in your browser’s address bar.
  • Only one app at a time can use the camera — close Zoom, Meet, etc.
  • If running locally, open via http://localhost — not file://.
  • Try Chrome, Edge, or Firefox on desktop.
